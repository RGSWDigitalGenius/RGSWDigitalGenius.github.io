Above, is the introduction video for the iBook G3 from 1999

Step into the time machine and travel back to 1999 when Apple unveiled its iconic iBook G3, a laptop that combined cutting-edge technology with a dash of colorful whimsy. The iBook G3, affectionately known as the "Clamshell" due to its distinctive design resembling a colorful clamshell, was a breath of fresh air in the tech world.


Boasting a 12.1-inch display, a speedy PowerPC G3 processor, and a curvaceous, translucent plastic exterior, it was a fashion statement as much as a computing powerhouse. Apple gave users the freedom to express their individuality by offering the iBook G3 in an array of vibrant colors, from Tangerine to Blueberry, turning heads in coffee shops and classrooms alike. Equipped with a handle for easy portability and a robust design that could withstand the bumps and bruises of everyday life, the iBook G3 wasn't just a laptop; it was a stylish companion for the tech-savvy adventurer. This iconic piece of tech history not only marked a turning point in laptop design but also became a beloved relic for those who fondly remember the days when their computing world was a little more colorful.
