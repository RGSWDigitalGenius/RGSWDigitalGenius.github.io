New members are always welcome. As an appreciation of dedicated attendance to Thursday break-time meetings, you will receive an official Digital Genius Badge with the DLP (Digital Learning Programme) logo printed on it.

As a Digital Leader, you will get the opportunity to attend the annual BETT Show held at ExCeL London. BETT is the world's largest Ed-Tech (educational technology) show dedicated to exhibiting new technology - and you'll be right at the forefront of it.

Please see the official BETT Show website for more information.
